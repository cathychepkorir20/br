Interactive live class

Interactive learning is a more hands-on, real-world process of relaying information in classrooms. Passive learning relies on listening to teachers lecture or rote memorization of information, figures, or equations. But with interactive learning, students are invited to participate in the conversation, through technology

  • Engagement- One big benefit of all types of interactive learning is the positive effect on student engagement.
  • Collaboration- In more passive, lecture-focused learning environments, one or two students typically dominate class discussions.
  • Critical thinking- Most importantly, interactive learning pushes students to stretch their abilities and gives them the tools to achieve deeper learning.
